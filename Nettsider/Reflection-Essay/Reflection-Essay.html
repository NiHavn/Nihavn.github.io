Managing and adjusting as a university student!

Code Assistance with Stack Overflow

Behind almost every programming student, no matter how competent they are, is a website called stackoverflow which assists you in understanding and implementing code. The major benefit of stackoverflow is the ability to find solutions in not only HTML / CSS / Javascript, but virtually any programming language that is out there. Simply google your problem and find a vast amount of explanations in order to assist you in understanding the code. Additionally, staying active in stack-overflow is a great way to expand your portfolio!

Managing the Workload

The biggest change that an incoming student will have to adapt to is the schedule. Although showing up is no longer mandatory, it is so incredibly important that a student shows up to lectures and seminars. The pacing of university syllabuses far exceed gynmnasium syllabuses and keeping a steady progress of your work is key to academic performance. It may sound tempting to simply drop the classes and seminars due to a lack of obligation, but this however, will likely lead to academic struggles. Manage your time efficiently and keep a close watch of your time schedule. It will frequently be updated in case a class is moved to another building or maybe postponed.
